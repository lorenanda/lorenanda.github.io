### *Never Split The Difference* by Chris Voss
ðŸŒ•ðŸŒ•ðŸŒ•ðŸŒ‘ðŸŒ‘  The author is a former international hostage negotiator for the FBI who seems to have thought that putting his self-boasting attitude on paper is the key to success. ### *No Filter* by Sarah Frier
